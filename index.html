    Our Story

    Made in phases. Small-batch, planet-considerate, skin-first. I formulate in tiny runs so everything you touch is fresh.

    Moons Market started with a single pen of cuticle oil and a notebook of moon charts. I believe your routine should feel like a ritual- simple, sensory, and tuned to where you’re at.

    Every jar and vial is blended with gentle, ingredient-forward bases and perfumed with subtle, mood-led accords. No parabens, no phthalates, no petrolatum- just thoughtful care for hands, lips, and skin.
